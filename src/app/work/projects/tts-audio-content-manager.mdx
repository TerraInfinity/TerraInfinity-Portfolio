---

## What It Does

This TTS SPA is more than just a text-to-speech converter—it’s a full audio creation platform. Here’s what makes it stand out:

- **Integrated Audio Effects**: Embed sound effects directly into your text with simple placeholders (e.g., `[CHIMES]`), creating dynamic, layered audio.  
- **Flexible Voice Options**: Choose from a variety of TTS engines—free or premium—to suit your budget and style.  
- **Modular Content**: Build your project with reusable sections, perfect for podcasts, meditations, or lessons.  
- **Easy Output**: Generate, preview, and download your audio in a few clicks, all within one intuitive interface.  

It’s designed to streamline workflows, cut costs, and unlock creativity for anyone who wants to tell a story through sound.

---

## How to Use It

Getting started is simple and intuitive:

1. **Setup**: Load the app in your browser and configure your preferences (e.g., TTS engine, audio storage).  
2. **Create**: Write your script, add sound effect placeholders, and assign voices to different sections.  
3. **Preview**: Generate draft audio to test your creation—tweak as needed.  
4. **Export**: Merge everything into a single, polished file and download it for your audience.  

Whether you’re crafting a 5-minute podcast or a 30-minute guided meditation, this tool adapts to your needs with minimal effort.
